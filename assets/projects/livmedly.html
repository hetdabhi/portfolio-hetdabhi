About This Project

LivMedly is a comprehensive medical and healthcare website designed to connect patients with healthcare services. The platform provides easy access to doctor appointments, medical information, and health resources.

Built with a focus on user-friendly navigation and accessibility, the website features appointment booking, doctor profiles, health articles, and emergency contact information. The clean, professional design ensures patients can quickly find the medical services they need.

Key Features

  • Multi-Role System: Patients, Doctors, and Administrators with dedicated dashboards
  • Appointment Booking: Easy scheduling and management of doctor consultations
  • Prescription Management: Digital prescriptions with secure tracking
  • Medical Departments: Neurology, Eye Care, Cardiac Care, Heart Care, Osteoporosis, ENT
  • Health Monitoring: BMI & health metrics tracking tools
  • Real-time Chat: Instant communication between patients and doctors
  • AI Chatbot: 24/7 virtual health assistance and guidance
  • Notifications: Automatic appointment reminders and health updates
  • Payment System: Integrated payment gateway for online consultations
  • Interactive Tools: BMI & Calorie Calculator, Discount Spin Wheel, and Health Tips
  • Admin Dashboard: User management, analytics, and system control
  • Security Features: Role-based access, data protection, and session security
  • Responsive Design: Fully mobile-friendly and cross-browser compatible

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge 1: Appointment Scheduling

Creating an intuitive appointment booking system that prevents double bookings and shows available time slots.

Solution: Implemented a calendar-based booking system with real-time availability checking and confirmation emails.

Challenge 2: Medical Information Accuracy

Ensuring all medical information and health articles are accurate, reliable, and easy to understand.

Solution: Collaborated with medical professionals for content review and implemented clear disclaimers.

What I Learned

  • Building healthcare-focused user interfaces
  • Implementing appointment booking systems
  • Working with date/time scheduling logic
  • Creating accessible designs for all users
  • Integrating Google Maps for location services
  • Form validation for medical information
  • Healthcare industry UX best practices
